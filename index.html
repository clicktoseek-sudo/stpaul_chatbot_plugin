🏫 About Our School

Saint Paul's School (SPS) is an English Medium Institution affiliated to CISCE, New Delhi. Established in 1999, the school is open to all irrespective of religion, caste, creed and community. Located on over five acres of established gardens in Denguajhar, Jalpaiguri.

The aim of the school is to mould young children for all-round development — academically, physically, mentally and aesthetically — in a homely atmosphere. Values of honesty, integrity and loyalty are instilled in every student along with self-respect, cleanliness and sense of responsibility.
